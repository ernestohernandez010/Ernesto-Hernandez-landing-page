Important Tools

Knowing what types of models exist is great. However the tools you need are just as important.

Cutting Snippers


The most used tool of all.

Cheaper IS NOT better.

Tweezers


The tool to handle small pieces and decals

Make sure to not bend them.

X-Acto Blades


When your snippers fail, you need to use something a bit sharper

Be careful with the sharp edges, it's hard to build without fingers.
